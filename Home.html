KING 810

King 810 is an American heavy metal band from Flint, Michigan, formed in 2007. The band currently consists of David Gunn and Eugene Gill, with touring musicians Johnpaul Vega and Hector Dominguez. The band's first release was their independent EP titled Midwest Monsters in 2012, which earned them a signing with Roadrunner Records they released their second EP titled Proem in 2014, and their debut studio album Memoirs of a Murderer that same year.

The band is known for its gritty stage presence and elaborate stage productions, with signature elements including Gunn performing covered in mud, hired men in masks wielding axes and baseball bats, and police security tape surrounding the stage to enhance the elements of crime and danger in their music. At times, they have been known to wield fake guns on stage. Some media outlets have criticized them as gimmicky and believe they are putting on an act, but Gunn rejects the idea that they are faking their stories. A December 2016 show in Chicago was cancelled due to "excessive use of guns and violent imagery".

Flint Michigan Water Crisis

King 810 have penned a benefit track titled “We Gotta Help Ourselves”. The song aims to raise awareness about the current water contamination crisis in the band's hometown of Flint, MI. A special t-shirt has been designed alongside the single, with proceeds to go towards the Flint Child Health & Development Fund.Frontman David Gunn said of the matter:We chose to work with the Flint Child Health & Development Fund because we believe children are the most important people in the equation. They're also affected the most by what's going on. Aside from the urgent need for clean water, it's important to focus on the long term effects that could come as a result to this exposure, so that's our main focus here.
